ABOUT MOTION.DK

Motion.dk is a movie resource website designed to provide insight into a part of Danish culture.

What Do We Actually Do?

Through our curated database of Danish films from the past two-decades, we offer the ability to explore our library and meet engage in our community driven forum.

Why Films?

Motion.DK uses films to provide a space to explore a part of Danish arts, gaining an insight into cultural norms as well as the language, whilst being entertained.

How Motion.dk Can Benefit You!

With many people moving to Denmark, understanding customs (or the language) can be a struggle. Through film, you can understand, in context, how language is used; social norms; as well as the stylist nature of Danish cinema. Additionally, engaging in the community forum, will allow you to gain recommendations from other expats, movie enthusiast and Danes to widen your knowledge.
